Critical Thinking Questions

1 .
What do you consider to be the three most critical global issues? Why?
2 .
In what ways is the ethnosphere valuable to our lives today?
3 .
Do you have any traditional forms of knowledge? If so, how do you use them? Think about practices such as cooking.
4 .
What anthropological skills can best address the problems our global community faces today?
5 .
What are the most valuable anthropological skills in today’s professions?
6 .
Why does anthropology matter?
7 .
Consider your own academic major or career goals. How can anthropological skills be applied to your professional aspirations?
8 .
How can anthropology lead in the future?
