  2. Find the probability that at least 150 favor a charter school.
  3. Find the probability that at most 160 favor a charter school.
  4. Find the probability that at most 160 favor a charter school.
  5. Find the probability that more than 155 favor a charter school.
  6. Find the probability that more than 155 favor a charter school.
  7. Find the probability that fewer than 147 favor a charter school.
  8. Find the probability that fewer than 147 favor a charter school.
  9. Find the probability that exactly 175 favor a charter school.
  10. Find the probability that exactly 175 favor a charter school.

Let X = the number that favor a charter school for grades K trough 5. X ~ B(n, p) where n = 300 and p = 0.53. Since np > 5 and nq > 5, use the normal approximation to the binomial. The formulas for the mean and standard deviation are μ = np and σ = npq npq . The mean is 159 and the standard deviation is 8.6447. The random variable for the normal distribution is Y. Y ~ N(159, 8.6447). See The Normal Distribution for help with calculator instructions.
